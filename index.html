Taco App

Eat-Da-Taco is a restaurant app that lets users input the names of tacos they'd like to eat. Whenever a user submits a taco's name, your app will display the taco on the left side of the page -- waiting to be devoured. Each taco in the waiting area also has a Devour it! button. When the user clicks it, the taco will move to the right side of the page. Your app will store every taco in a MySQL database, whether devoured or not.
